Our Educational Mission

Our work is grounded in the foundational ideas of great economic and philosophical thinkers, from the moral sentiments of Adam Smith to the free-market principles of Milton Friedman and the ethical frameworks of Immanuel Kant. We bridge theory with practice by learning from modern titans of industry and academia.

Our flagship business course, now hosted in select public libraries, provides accessible, high-level business education to aspiring entrepreneurs everywhere. For inquiries about our curriculum, please feel free to email us.

Course Highlights:

  • Core Economic Principles: From macro to micro.
  • Entrepreneurial Strategy: Market analysis and validation.
  • Leadership & Ethics: Building sustainable and responsible ventures.
